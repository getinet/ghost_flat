UX & UI Design

As the lead UX/UI designer for Flite Design Studio, I played a key role in shaping the product’s design and user experience. I worked closely with my UX team to create high-fidelity HTML prototypes, which served as the foundation for our engineering team’s development work.

Our mission was to give designers greater flexibility in building rich, interactive web ads—directly in the browser. We wanted the experience to feel as intuitive and powerful as traditional creative tools like Adobe Photoshop. To achieve this, we focused on direct manipulation, drag-and-drop interactions, and context-aware panels. A key innovation was refining our component-based architecture, making it easier for users to integrate functionality like Twitter feeds, YouTube videos, and interactive polls with minimal effort.

Launched in 2012, Design Studio set the UX standard for our entire product suite. It was well received by customers and consistently praised for its thoughtful, intuitive design.
